About Web Dev Quiz Adventure Game

Web Dev Quiz Adventure is a thrilling quiz game that challenges your web development skills. Test your knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript through a series of progressively difficult questions across five levels. How to Play: Answer multiple-choice questions to advance throughlevels. Failing to choose the correct answer results instant death. Choose from a set of doors, some leading to rewards and others to trap doors that reduce your lives. Successfully navigate through all levels to win, or avoid losing all your lives. Features: Levels: Five levels of increasing difficulty. Interactive Doors: Decipher which doors are safe and which are traps. Themes: Switch between light and dark modes. Challenge yourself, track your high scores, and enjoy the ultimate web development quiz experience!
